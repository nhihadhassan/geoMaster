# GeoMaster Instagram Story Promo Pack

Phase 1 deliverable for Instagram Stories and vertical promo assets.

## Production Defaults

- Format: Instagram Story, `1080x1920`.
- Safe area: keep essential text and CTA inside the center `900x1500`.
- Brand assets: use `public/brand/geomaster-globe.png` for hero marks and `public/brand/geomaster-icon-192.png` for small lockups.
- Product URL: `geo-master-livid.vercel.app`.
- Visual language: premium dark atlas, blue globe, Mapbox globe screenshots, glass HUD panels, cyan discovery accents, emerald success accents.
- Typography: Geist or a close modern sans. Use bold, compact headlines and short supporting copy.
- Motion if animated: 150 to 300ms UI transitions, slow globe drift, quick map-fill moment. Avoid bouncy or cartoon motion.

## Story QA Checklist

- Text is readable on a phone at arm's length.
- One main idea per frame.
- Logo/icon appears, but does not crowd the screenshot.
- CTA is visible in the lower third, above Instagram UI controls.
- Screenshots show real GeoMaster UI or clearly marked placeholders.
- Color states stay consistent: emerald correct, amber assisted, rose missed.
- No fake features, no unsupported claims, no generic stock globe visuals.
