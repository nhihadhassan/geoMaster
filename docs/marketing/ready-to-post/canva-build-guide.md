# GeoMaster Canva Build Guide

Production guide for building the Instagram Story promo, demo end card, and tall marketing cards in Canva.

## Canva Dimensions

- Instagram Story: `1080 x 1920 px`
- Instagram Reel cover: `1080 x 1920 px`
- Instagram feed portrait: `1080 x 1350 px`
- Square fallback: `1080 x 1080 px`

## Recommended Canva Setup

1. Create a new design at `1080 x 1920 px`.
2. Add guides:
   - Outer margin: `72 px`
   - Inner safe content area: roughly `900 x 1500 px`
   - Bottom CTA zone: keep above the lowest `260 px`
3. Upload:
   - `public/brand/geomaster-globe.png`
   - `public/brand/geomaster-icon-192.png`
   - App screenshots or screen recordings.
4. Create reusable elements:
   - GeoMaster icon lockup.
   - Rounded phone screenshot frame.
   - Emerald CTA button.
   - Small mode chips.

## Font Suggestions

Use whichever is available in your Canva account.

- Primary: `Geist`, `Inter`, `Satoshi`, or `Manrope`
- Headline fallback: `Montserrat SemiBold` or `Aptos Display`
- Body: same family as headline, regular or medium weight
- Numeric/timer details: `Geist Mono`, `Roboto Mono`, or `IBM Plex Mono`

Use one font family per asset when possible. Keep it product-clean.

## Color Palette

Use these as Canva hex values.

- Deep atlas: `#020617`
- Zinc glass: `#09090B`
- Slate mist: `#E2E8F0`
- Text white: `#F8FAFC`
- Muted text: `#CBD5E1`
- Electric cyan: `#22D3EE`
- Deep cyan: `#0369A1`
- Correct emerald: `#34D399`
- Assisted amber: `#FBBF24`
- Missed rose: `#FB7185`

Opacity guidance:

- Glass panel fill: 45 to 72 percent.
- Borders: 10 to 22 percent white or cyan.
- Background map screenshot: 30 to 65 percent brightness, depending on text readability.

## Story Layout Directions

### Launch Story Frames

- Place the headline in the upper third.
- Place screenshot or phone mockup in the center.
- Put CTA in the lower third, above Instagram controls.
- Use the blue globe icon as a small top or bottom brand mark.
- Keep each frame to one main message.

### Screenshot Placement

- Idle globe screenshot: full-bleed background with dark overlay.
- Type-to-Fill screenshot: phone frame tilted 6 to 12 degrees.
- Mode montage: three stacked rounded screenshot cards.
- Country facts: floating glass card over a calm map crop.
- CTA end card: centered logo, screenshot strip, URL.

## Marketing Card Layout Directions

Use these for the four rounded promo cards inspired by the reference image.

### Card 1: Learn The World Through The Map

- Headline: upper left, 2 lines max.
- Visual: tilted phone mockup in the lower half.
- Background: dark globe crop with subtle cyan light.
- Footer: GeoMaster icon plus URL.

### Card 2: Can You Name Every Country?

- Headline: upper left.
- Visual: Type-to-Fill phone screenshot, large and central.
- Accent: emerald success chip or progress line.
- Footer: `Start a quiz`

### Card 3: Guess From Capitals, Shapes, And Map Clicks

- Headline: upper left.
- Visual: three stacked screenshots, slightly offset.
- Accent: cyan mode labels.
- Footer: `Pick a mode`

### Card 4: Explore Stories Behind Every Country

- Headline: upper left.
- Visual: large country education card.
- Background: softly blurred map.
- Footer: `Explore Learning Mode`

## Effects And Styling

- Rounded card corners: 56 to 72px.
- Phone frame corner radius: 36 to 48px.
- Screenshot shadow: soft black shadow at 20 to 35 percent opacity.
- Avoid heavy outer neon glow. Use subtle cyan light behind the blue globe icon only.
- Avoid gradient text. Use solid white or cyan text.
- Do not use peach/pastel travel colors from the reference. Borrow the card structure only.

## Export Settings

### Story/Reel

- File type: MP4 for animated/video, PNG for static.
- Resolution: `1080 x 1920`.
- Quality: highest available.
- Frame rate for video: 30fps.

### Feed Carousel

- File type: PNG.
- Resolution: `1080 x 1350`.
- Export each card as a separate image.
- Name files:
  - `geomaster-card-01-map.png`
  - `geomaster-card-02-countries.png`
  - `geomaster-card-03-modes.png`
  - `geomaster-card-04-stories.png`

## Final QA

- Text is readable at phone size.
- No important text sits under Instagram UI.
- URL is visible for CTA frames.
- Screenshots are real or clearly marked placeholders.
- Colors match GeoMaster states: emerald correct, amber assisted, rose missed.
- The design feels like a premium atlas/game, not a classroom quiz worksheet.
