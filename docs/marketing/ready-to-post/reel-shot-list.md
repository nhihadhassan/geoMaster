# GeoMaster Reel Shot List

Ready-to-capture plan for a 15 second vertical demo reel.

## Export Target

- Format: `1080x1920`, 9:16.
- Length: 15 seconds.
- Recording source: live site or local app.
- Recommended capture viewport: mobile width around `390px` or `400px`, then place in a phone frame.
- Pace: crisp and premium, not frantic.

## Exact Screen Recordings To Capture

### Clip 1: Idle Globe

- Duration in final edit: `0:00-0:02`
- Capture length: 3 to 4 seconds.
- App state: Learning Mode / idle globe, compact GeoMaster HUD visible.
- Action: slowly drag or let the globe sit still if it already looks calm.
- On-screen caption: `Learn the world through the map.`
- Edit note: add a slight digital push-in, about 103 percent.

### Clip 2: Choose Quiz

- Duration in final edit: `0:02-0:04`
- Capture length: 3 seconds.
- App state: tap `Quiz` or `Choose Quiz`.
- Action: open the region/mode selector.
- On-screen caption: `Pick a region.`
- Edit note: cut as the drawer/sheet settles.

### Clip 3: Type-To-Fill Payoff

- Duration in final edit: `0:04-0:07`
- Capture length: 5 to 7 seconds.
- App state: Type-to-Fill quiz running.
- Action: type a recognizable country such as `Brazil`, `United States`, or `France`.
- Must show: answer input, map fill turning green, compact correct feedback.
- On-screen caption: `Type. Guess. Fill the map.`
- Edit note: trim tightly around the green map-fill moment.

### Clip 4: Mode Variety

- Duration in final edit: `0:07-0:09`
- Capture length: 2 to 4 seconds per mode, then cut quickly.
- App states to capture:
  - Identify-the-Shaded prompt.
  - Map Click prompt.
  - Capital Challenge prompt.
- On-screen caption: `Shapes. Capitals. Map clicks.`
- Edit note: use three fast cuts, each about 0.6 to 0.7 seconds.

### Clip 5: Country Facts

- Duration in final edit: `0:09-0:12`
- Capture length: 4 to 5 seconds.
- App state: Learning Mode country card open.
- Action: click or tap a country and show the facts card.
- Must show: country name, flag, capital, stats or fun fact.
- On-screen caption: `Then learn the story behind it.`
- Edit note: gentle push-in on the country card.

### Clip 6: CTA End Card

- Duration in final edit: `0:12-0:15`
- Capture length: static design, no screen recording required.
- Visual: blue globe icon, GeoMaster wordmark, screenshot strip.
- On-screen caption: `Play GeoMaster free.`
- URL: `geo-master-livid.vercel.app`
- Edit note: hold for at least 2 seconds so the URL is readable.

## Music And Sound Suggestion

- Style: minimal electronic, soft pulse, clean atlas/game feel.
- Tempo: 90 to 115 BPM.
- Avoid: cartoon sound effects, heavy trap drops, loud whooshes, classroom-style jingles.
- If using app sound effects: keep music very low and let correct-answer chimes be audible.
- If silent: captions should fully explain the reel.

## Capture Checklist

- Clear browser/app chrome if possible.
- Use mobile viewport or real phone recording.
- Keep the input, HUD, and correct feedback visible.
- Do not show debug panels or dev tools.
- Avoid long pauses while typing.
- Capture at least one clean green-fill success moment.
