# GeoMaster Story Demo Video Script

Phase 1 short demo reel for Instagram Stories, Reels, and vertical launch posts.

## Demo Format

- Canvas: `1080x1920`, 9:16.
- Duration: 15 seconds target, acceptable range 12 to 18 seconds.
- Source footage: screen recording from the live site or local app.
- Music/sound: optional subtle click/chime bed. No busy background music if the screen recording already has quiz feedback sounds.
- Editing style: crisp cuts, gentle zooms, no spinning text, no comic effects.
- CTA URL: `geo-master-livid.vercel.app`.

## Recommended 15 Second Storyboard

| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Voiceover or caption |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0:00-0:02 | Idle GeoMaster globe with clean HUD and blue icon. | `Learn the world through the map.` | `Meet GeoMaster.` |
| 0:02-0:04 | Tap `Choose Quiz`, region/mode sheet opens. | `Pick a region.` | `Choose a region and a quiz mode.` |
| 0:04-0:07 | Type `Brazil` or `United States`, map fills green. | `Type. Guess. Fill the map.` | `Name countries and watch the map respond.` |
| 0:07-0:09 | Show Identify or Map Click prompt. | `Or play by shape and location.` | `Switch to shapes, map clicks, or capitals.` |
| 0:09-0:12 | Country success card or education card appears. | `Then learn the story behind it.` | `Open country facts, capitals, languages, and stories.` |
| 0:12-0:15 | End card with blue globe icon, app screenshot strip, URL. | `Play GeoMaster free.` | `Play free at geo-master-livid.vercel.app.` |

## Shot List

### Shot 1: Atlas Open

- Capture: landing closed, idle Learning Mode globe visible.
- Camera/edit: slow 103% digital zoom toward globe center.
- Text: `Learn the world through the map.`
- Notes: let the app feel premium before showing controls.

### Shot 2: Choose Quiz

- Capture: tap `Quiz` or `Choose Quiz`, show region/mode drawer.
- Camera/edit: quick cut, no exaggerated zoom.
- Text: `Choose a region.`
- Notes: show the selector briefly, not long enough to feel like a settings tutorial.

### Shot 3: Type-to-Fill Payoff

- Capture: start South America or North America, type a country, show green fill and compact correct feedback.
- Camera/edit: cut on the moment the map turns green.
- Text: `Name countries. Fill the map.`
- Notes: this is the main dopamine moment.

### Shot 4: Mode Variety

- Capture: fast montage of Identify, Map Click, Capital Challenge.
- Camera/edit: three clips, about 0.7 seconds each.
- Text: `Shapes. Capitals. Map clicks.`
- Notes: keep labels large and sparse.

### Shot 5: Learning Card

- Capture: click a country in Learning Mode, open education card.
- Camera/edit: gentle push-in on facts card.
- Text: `Facts, languages, GDP, stories.`
- Notes: make this feel like an atlas, not trivia clutter.

### Shot 6: CTA End Card

- Capture: static branded card, no app interaction needed.
- Camera/edit: hold 2 to 3 seconds.
- Text: `Play GeoMaster free.`
- Subtext: `geo-master-livid.vercel.app`
- Notes: include blue globe icon and one screenshot strip.

## Alternate 18 Second Cut

Use the 15 second version, then add:

- 0:15-0:17: `Whole World` challenge teaser.
- 0:17-0:18: final URL pulse.

## On-Screen Text Rules

- Maximum 7 words per text beat.
- Keep text high contrast: white or cyan on dark map.
- Use one CTA only on the final frame.
- Avoid emoji, meme captions, and shouty punctuation.
- Do not cover the input dock, HUD timer, or correct toast in screen recordings.

## Voiceover Options

### Clean Product Voiceover

`GeoMaster is a map-first geography game. Choose a region, name countries, guess capitals, tap the map, and explore the stories behind every place. Play free at geo-master-livid.vercel.app.`

### Challenge Voiceover

`Think you know the world? Pick a region, start the timer, and fill the map one country at a time. When you are ready, take on the Whole World quiz.`

### Quiet Premium Voiceover

`No flashcards. No clutter. Just a beautiful map, fast geography challenges, and country facts that make the world easier to remember.`

## Design Tool Prompt

`Create a 15 second 9:16 Instagram reel storyboard for GeoMaster, a premium map-first geography web app. Use dark atlas visuals, the blue globe icon, real app screenshot placeholders, crisp screen recording beats, and sparse white/cyan text. Show: idle globe, choose quiz, type a country, country turns green, mode variety, country facts card, final CTA "Play GeoMaster free" and "geo-master-livid.vercel.app". Keep motion restrained and premium, not childish.`

## Editing QA Checklist

- First 2 seconds clearly explain why to watch.
- The green map-fill moment is visible.
- At least three app features appear without feeling rushed.
- The URL is readable for at least 2 seconds.
- No text is hidden by Instagram UI chrome.
- The final export works without sound, with captions alone.
