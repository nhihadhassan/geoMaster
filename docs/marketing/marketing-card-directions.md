# GeoMaster Marketing Card Directions

Phase 1 tall marketing cards inspired by the provided travel-app reference.

## Card System

- Format: vertical card, `1080x1920` for Stories or `1080x1350` for feed portrait.
- Layout inspiration: rounded standalone cards with large headline, angled phone/mockup, map texture, and one clear product benefit.
- GeoMaster adaptation: use dark atlas surfaces, blue globe identity, cyan/emerald accents, real app screenshots, and less pastel softness.
- Corner radius: generous, around 56 to 72px for card art.
- Safe margins: 72px outside, 56px inside.
- Screenshot style: phone frame, tilted panel, or floating glass UI screenshot. Keep screenshots legible.
- Avoid: peach travel palette, generic pin icons, emoji, fake map UI, cluttered fact blocks.

## Shared Visual Ingredients

- Background: deep zinc or slate with faint map/globe texture.
- Accent: cyan glow from the GeoMaster icon, emerald success line or filled-country highlight.
- Brand mark: small blue globe icon near a corner or in a clean footer.
- CTA footer: `Play free` plus `geo-master-livid.vercel.app`.
- Type: large bold headline, short support line, small mode badge if helpful.

## Card 1: Learn The World Through The Map

- Headline: `Learn the world through the map`

- Screenshot needed: idle Learning Mode globe with compact HUD.
- Composition:
  - Top third: headline, left aligned.
  - Middle: tilted phone mockup, overlapping a faint globe crop.
  - Bottom: small GeoMaster icon, URL, and one cyan line.
- Feed caption: `A geography app should feel like the world, not a worksheet. GeoMaster is live.`
- Design prompt: `Design a premium vertical marketing card for GeoMaster. Use a dark atlas background, large headline "Learn the world through the map", tilted phone mockup with the GeoMaster globe HUD, blue globe icon glow, cyan accents, and a small CTA "Play free" with "geo-master-livid.vercel.app". Inspired by rounded app promo cards, but dark, refined, and map-first.`

## Story Version Direction

For Story exports, convert each card into a full-screen asset:

- Increase headline size by 12 to 18 percent.
- Move CTA above the bottom Instagram controls.
- Keep screenshot centered and large.
- Remove secondary chips if the frame feels crowded.

## Design QA Checklist

- The product screenshot is the hero, not decoration.
- The card can be understood in under 2 seconds.
- The headline does not exceed 2 lines unless absolutely necessary.
- Dark areas are not pure black; use tinted zinc/slate.
- Accent color is restrained and purposeful.
- No generic map pins unless they appear inside real app UI.
- CTA and URL are readable at phone size.
